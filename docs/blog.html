Meta-Analysis Magic

Welcome to my blog, Meta-Analysis Magic! Here, you’ll uncover mind-bending tricks that will help you unveil those elusive, unreported statistics tucked away in primary studies. But that’s not all – we’ll also dive into the thrilling realm of lesser-known meta-analysis modeling techniques that tend to slip under the radar.

To make your journey even more enchanting, each post will come complete with R code, making it a breeze for you to work your own meta-analysis wizardry. Let’s embark on this adventure together!
